About Us

Welcome to Moksu Mung Fried Chicken, where our passion for crispy, flavorful fried chicken comes to life. Our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the best fried chicken experience to our community.

Founded by Moksu Mung, our restaurant has grown from a small food stall to a beloved local eatery known for its delicious and perfectly seasoned fried chicken. Every piece of chicken is prepared with care, using a secret family recipe that has been passed down for generations.

At Moksu Mung Fried Chicken, we believe in using only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods to ensure every bite is as satisfying as the last. Whether you're dining in with family and friends or grabbing a quick meal to go, we're committed to providing an exceptional culinary experience.

Thank you for choosing Moksu Mung Fried Chicken. We look forward to serving you soon!
